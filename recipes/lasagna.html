Lasagna

EASIEST LASAGNA EVER

12 Servings
prep time:
20 Minutes
cook time:
45 Minutes
total time:
1 Hour 5 Minutes

A dish that is easy to prepare on a week night.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Koster salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste1
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 (15-ounce) package whole milk ricotta
  • 3 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella, devided
  • 1 large egg, beaten1
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 deg.lightly oil a baking dish or coat with nonstick spray.

  2. In a large pot of boileing salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to packet instructions.

  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add ground beef and onions and cook until beef has browned, about 3-5 minutes,making sure to crumble the beef as it cooks; season with salt and pepper,to taste. Drain exstra fat. Strir in tomatoes and italian seasoning until well combined.

  4. In a medium bowl,combine ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella according egg;set asside.

  5. Spread one cup of tomato mixture onto the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish;top with 3 lasagna noodles,1/2 of ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup mozarella cheese. Repeat with a second layer.Top with remaining noodles,tomato mixture, 1 cup mozarella cheese and parmesan.

  6. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots.

  7. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve,garnisged with parsley, if desired.
